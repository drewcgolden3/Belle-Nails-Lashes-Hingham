Our Story

A Hingham staple,
built for beauty.

Belle Nails & Lashes has become a go-to destination right in the heart of Hingham's downtown at 284 Main Street. Offering a full menu of nail and lash services — from classic manicures and luxurious spa pedicures to intricate nail art, acrylics, and full lash extensions — the team at Belle is committed to making every client feel their best.

Reviews consistently praise the friendly, skilled technicians and the welcoming, clean environment. Walk-ins are always welcome, but appointments are recommended so you get the time and attention you deserve.

What We Offer

The full menu

From everyday maintenance to special-occasion looks. Nails, lashes, and everything in between.

Classic Manicure
Nail shaping, cuticle care, and your choice of polish color. The timeless standard.
Gel Manicure
Long-lasting gel polish cured under UV — chip-free for weeks. Available in dozens of shades.
Dip Powder Manicure
Durable color with a natural finish. Great for clients who want strength without acrylics.
Nail Art
Custom designs, patterns, and embellishments. From subtle accents to full intricate art.
French Manicure
The classic white-tip look — clean, elegant, and enduringly popular.
Polish Change
Remove existing polish and apply fresh color. Quick and easy refresh.
Classic Pedicure
Soak, nail shaping, cuticle care, callus work, massage, and polish.
Gel Pedicure
All the pampering of a classic pedicure, with long-lasting gel color that won't chip.
Spa Pedicure
An elevated experience with exfoliating scrub, mask treatment, and an extended massage.
Deluxe Pedicure
Premium treatment with extra massage time, paraffin wax, and full relaxation.
Full Set Acrylics
New acrylic extensions applied to all ten nails — shaped and finished to your preference.
Acrylic Fill
Fill in the grown-out area and freshen the color on your existing acrylic set.
Gel Extensions
Builder gel applied for lighter, more flexible length. A popular alternative to acrylics.
Nail Repair
Fix a broken nail or cracked extension — single-nail repairs available.
Classic Lash Extensions
One extension per natural lash for a defined, mascara-like look. Perfect for a natural lift.
Volume Lash Extensions
Multiple lightweight extensions per lash for a full, dramatic, fluffy finish.
Lash Fill
Refresh and fill in grown-out extensions to restore fullness. Recommended every 2–3 weeks.
Lash Lift & Tint
Lift and curl your natural lashes from the root, then tint for depth. No extensions required.
Brow Shaping
Precise shaping and tidying of your natural brows for a clean, flattering frame.
Brow Tint
Semi-permanent tint adds depth and definition to sparse or light brows.
Eyebrow Wax
Clean, flattering shaping using warm wax for precise hair removal.
Upper Lip Wax
Quick and precise removal for smooth, clean skin.
Full Face Wax
Comprehensive facial hair removal for a smooth, even complexion.
Chin & Neck Wax
Targeted removal for a clean chin and neckline.
